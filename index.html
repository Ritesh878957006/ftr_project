Percentage Conversion

Here are the steps to convert percentage to CGPA:
1. First, divide the percentage value by 9.5.
2. Applying the formula: (Percentage Value / 9.5) = Final Value (CGPA). For example, if your percentage is 85.5, then your CGPA would be 85.5 / 9.5 = 9.

Here are the steps to convert percentage to SGPA:
1. First, divide the percentage value by 10 and then add 0.75 with it1.
2. Applying the formula: (Percentage Value / 10) + 0.75 = Final Value (SGPA)1. For example, if your percentage is 85.5, then your SGPA would be (85.5 / 10) + 0.75 = 9.31.
